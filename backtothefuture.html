BACK TO THE FUTURE

Back to the Future: Teenager Marty McFly embarks on a wild time-traveling adventure with his eccentric scientist friend Doc Brown. A jolt of lightning sends Marty hurtling back to 1955, where he accidentally disrupts his parents' first meeting, jeopardizing his own existence. To set things right, Marty must navigate the teenage social scene of the past, ensure his parents fall in love, and find a way to return to his own time period before it's too late. Packed with humor, iconic characters, and thrilling adventure.
