JURASSIC PARK

Jurassic Park: Eccentric billionaire John Hammond unveils the ultimate theme park experience: living dinosaurs brought back to life through genetic engineering. A group of invited experts, including paleontologists Alan Grant and Ellie Sattler, are thrilled but cautious as they tour the park's wonders. However, chaos erupts when a power outage leads to the dinosaurs escaping their enclosures. The group must now fight for survival amidst rampaging predators, navigating a thrilling adventure fraught with danger and ethical dilemmas. "Jurassic Park" is a landmark film that blends science fiction, adventure, and suspense, exploring the consequences of scientific advancements and the fascination (and fear) of encountering prehistoric giants.
