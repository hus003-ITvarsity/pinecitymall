GHOST BUSTERS

Ghostbusters: Three down-on-their-luck scientists lose their cushy university jobs due to their obsession with the unseen. Undeterred, they become ghostbusting entrepreneurs, armed with wacky inventions and quick wit. As New York City groans under a spectral siege, these unlikely heroes face escalating paranormal threats, eventually confronting a powerful cosmic entity. Blending humor, sci-fi, and spooks, this classic adventure celebrates science, friendship, and conquering fear with a laugh.
